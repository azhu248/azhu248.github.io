Dear Angie,

Words cannot do justice my feelings for you, but I will try anyways. Your eyes shine like the stars themselves, and your presence warms me like the sun. My life before I met you felt like a night without stars, and I am grateful for every moment we spend together.

I thank God for bringing someone as perfect as you into my life every day. Even if I were to write a thousand letters, I would never figure out the proper configuration of words to express my love for you, but I hope you gleaned a small portion of it from this letter.

Love,
Andy
